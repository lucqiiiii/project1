One thousand people have applied to volunteer at the Champions League final in Cardiff The Football Association of Wales launched its Become a Champion campaign just two weeks ago It needs people to help with transport marketing ticketing and VIP services in the days before and on the final at the Principality Stadium on third June Were delighted by the response over the past weeks the committee leading the volunteering said Another people could perform in a prematch ceremony at the stadium  which will be called the National Stadium of Wales for the game Volunteers are also being sought for the Women Champions League final at the Cardiff City Stadium on June first It shows that the nation is really embracing the finals and wants to be a part of the action said Laura Easton the Local Organizing Committees head of volunteering Applications to express an interest in volunteering are still open
